Service 06

Data Protection

Your data is your most valuable asset. We design and implement comprehensive backup and disaster recovery strategies to ensure your data is always available, always protected, and always recoverable when you need it most.

  • Automated backup solutions
  • Offsite & cloud backup replication
  • Disaster recovery planning (DRP)
  • Business continuity planning (BCP)
  • Regular restore testing

Protection Pillars

3-2-1 Backup Strategy

Three copies of data, two media types, one offsite location — the industry gold standard for data resilience.

Rapid Recovery

Tested recovery procedures mean you can restore operations in hours, not days, after an incident.

Ransomware Protection

Immutable backups and air-gapped copies ensure ransomware can never encrypt your last line of defence.
